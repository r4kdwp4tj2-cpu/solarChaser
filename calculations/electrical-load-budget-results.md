## Interpretation limits

- All load values are engineering estimates until measured against selected equipment and use profiles.
- Space heating, water heating, air conditioning, fixed electric cooking, and vehicle traction are excluded.
- The 230 V peak is an operating envelope, not permission to use all possible appliances concurrently.
- Converter and inverter nameplates require transient, temperature, protection, cable, and diversity analysis.
- Solar-array and charger sizing require seasonal generation and mission-profile analyses.
