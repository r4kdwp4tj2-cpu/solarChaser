# SC-100 System Requirements Specification

Status: Draft Rev.0.1

This document will contain all Functional (FR) and Non-functional (NFR)
requirements together with traceability.

Current accepted highlights:
- Two permanent beds (1800x700 mm each)
- Toilet, no indoor shower
- Outside shower
- Fresh water: 40–50 L usable
- Grey water >=80% of fresh water
- 48 V house backbone
- 12 V derived service bus
- Solar priority, traction backup, shore on captain's command
- Remaining payload >=500 kg
- 20-year platform design life
